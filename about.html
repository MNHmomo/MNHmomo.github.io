About()

Bash-Breach is a blog, an indie web site, and a place for hosting my cybersecurity journey. This place in the internet is where I’ll document everything I’m learning, researching, and building. I’ll discuss about a range of cybersecurity topics and news, the latest security tools and trends, the best learning resources I use, and projects I’ve built and how I’ve built them.

This website isn’t just for journaling my experience, but also for you —especially if you’re a local student or just someone interested in cybersecurity.



/WhoAmI>_

My name is Mohamed Benaissa. I am an undergraduate freshman at the University of New Haven, pursuing a degree in Cybersecurity and Networks, with a concentration in network security. My interest in cybersecurity has stemmed from modding video games, trying to test the limit on how much I can configure and manipulate native game files.

I’m pursing a career in cybersecurity because it combines that technical, administrative, and configurative skill with critical thinking to make an impact in the digital world. Whether that is safeguarding data, creating and managing secure networks, and protecting your responsibilities from malware/viruses/hackers.
