FrigidStealer Malware Targets MacOS

A new macOS malware, FrigidStealer, is being spread via fake browser update scams by threat actor TA2727, targeting users outside North America. Delivered through compromised websites, it tricks victims into entering their system password via AppleScript, allowing it to steal browser data, Apple Notes, and cryptocurrency info. The malware, written in Go and built with WailsIO, bypasses Gatekeeper and appears legitimate. This comes amid a rise in macOS malware, including Tiny FUD and Flesh Stealer, which focus on evasion and persistence.
